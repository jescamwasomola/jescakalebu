EASTC CONVOCATION CEREMONY

Welcome to the 7th ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING OF CONVOCATION!

The EASTC convocation ceremony is a momentous event that celebrates the achievements and hard work of our graduates. It is a time-honored tradition where we come together to recognize accomplishments and mark the beginning of a new journey. With a rich history and a deep sense of pride, the convocation ceremony holds a special place in the hearts of our community.

The seventh convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of year 2024 continues the tradition with academic institutions of gathering together academicians, alumni,benefactorsand industry professionals as stskeholders of the university community.It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research and professional development.

Topic:

"The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania.

Theme:

Modernising Data Ecosystem to Accelerate the Implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA): The Role of Official Statistics and Big Data in the Economic Transformation and Sustainable Development in Tanzania.

Venue:

EASTC Function Hall

Date: 13th December, 2024

Objectives:

  • Encourage and promote spirit of unity among members.
  • Create in the alumni a sense of responsibility and accountability towards the community especially to the university.
  • Maintain dialogue with the government on matters concerning welfare of public.
  • Award prizes to alumni who have made significant achievement or contribution to their community and to the university.
  • Discuss any matter within sphere and competence of university.
